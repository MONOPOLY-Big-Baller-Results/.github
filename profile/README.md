## 🎱 How MONOPOLY Big Baller works — balls, cards and odds

MONOPOLY Big Baller is Evolution's **live bingo game show**, launched in 2022. Each round you take one to four 5×5 cards filled with numbers from 1 to 60, and the host starts the machine to draw **20 of the 60 numbered balls**. Matched numbers are daubed automatically and completed horizontal, vertical or diagonal lines pay out. Before the draw, MR. MONOPOLY pulls his lever to scatter random **free spaces** and **multipliers** across the cards. Two extra bet spots — **3 Rolls** and **5 Rolls** — are the keys to the 3D **MONOPOLY board Bonus Game**.

| Bet spot | How it works | Payout / role |
|---|---|---|
| Free Space card | 5×5 card, centre cell is a free space | 2:1 – 39:1 per completed line |
| Chance card | 5×5 card, centre holds a guaranteed multiplier | 2:1 – 199:1 per completed line |
| 3 Rolls 🎲 | 3 unique numbers — all must be drawn | Bonus Game with 3 dice rolls |
| 5 Rolls 🎲🎲 | 4 unique numbers — all must be drawn | Bonus Game with 5 dice rolls |

- **Every number** on a card has a 20-in-60 chance of coming out — exactly **33.3 %** per round.
- Base odds of landing the **3 Rolls** bonus work out to (20·19·18)/(60·59·58) ≈ **3.3 %** — about **1 in 30** rounds, before any random free spaces help.
- Base odds of the **5 Rolls** bonus ≈ **1.0 %** — roughly **1 in 101** rounds; random free spaces can only shorten those odds, never worsen them.
- The optimal published **RTP** is **96.10 %**; the Rolls bets are far more volatile from round to round.

> ⚠️ Every percentage above is a **theoretical long-run average**. Ball draws have no memory: a bonus that has been quiet for a long streak is **not** "due" to appear. The counters and alerts in this bot describe what has already happened — never what happens next. Any service claiming to predict the coming draw is misleading you.

## 🎩 The MONOPOLY board Bonus Game

When every number on an active 3 Rolls or 5 Rolls bet is drawn, the stream cuts to a 3D **MONOPOLY board** laid out just like the classic game — except every property carries a multiplier. Houses and hotels are built on random properties at the start, pushing those values higher. The dice are then rolled three or five times, and MR. MONOPOLY walks the board collecting each multiplier he lands on. Stopping on **Chance** or **Community Chest** can award a random cash prize, while **Go to Jail** marches him straight to the jail square. If both Rolls bets complete in the same round, two separate Bonus Games are played back to back.

## ✖️ Three kinds of multipliers

- **Standard multipliers (10x–20x)** sit on individual numbers — when such a number becomes part of a winning line, the line's payout is boosted.
- **Line multipliers (20x–50x)** apply when you complete the marked line itself.
- **Global multipliers (2x–3x)** lift all winnings on the slip, and different multiplier types can stack together on the same round.
